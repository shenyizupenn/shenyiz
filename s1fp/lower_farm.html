The Flexible Area is dedicated to fostering community engagement and personal reflection, emphasizing the project's holistic approach to design and its focus on the human-nature bond. The adoption of a "designing in layers" strategy for the farm's layout maximizes diversity and productivity by categorizing plant species according to their environmental needs, facilitating a balanced ecosystem.
Crop rotation practices further exemplify the project's ecological mindfulness, with seasonal rotations like field peas and sweet potatoes enhancing soil health and crop yield. This thoughtful approach to agriculture not only supports ecological balance but also promotes a sustainable, productive farming system that enriches the local community and environment, embodying the project's vision of ecological coexistence and permaflow gardens.
